Relationship Insight: "When Others First Didn't Matter"

Before the split: Relationships were fluid and story-free. People just were, without filters of who mattered.

After the split: A story entered - "me versus them" - creating separation and protection.

What can be re-included now:

  • Curiosity before judgment
  • Mutual mattering - making space for others to count
  • Present-time presence - meeting people as they are now
  • Balanced listening and speaking

Autobiography Timeline

Early Childhood - The Time Before

Before "others didn't matter" - natural inclusion, no mental filters, relationships just were.

Key learning: This natural state of inclusion can be reclaimed.

The Split - When Others Stopped Mattering

First sibling/neighbor incident creating "me vs them" - the birth of protection and separation.

Intelligent strategy then: Protection from hurt through disconnection.

Pattern Formation

Repeated experiences reinforcing the story that "others don't matter" to avoid disappointment.

Cost: Limited intimacy, empathy, and inclusion.

Present - Integration & Healing

Wisdom Course work: Distinguishing outdated strategies and creating new ways of being.

New possibility: "If it's there for me, it's there for them" - empathy and inclusion.

Future Vision

Living as author of life, not victim of circumstances. Full expression across all spheres.
